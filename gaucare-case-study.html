Project Overview

Not a Cattle SaaS. A Dairy Income Engine.

Gau Care is built on a foundational insight: farmers don't want dashboards or analytics — they want to know what to do today. So we built a decision engine, not a data app.

The Problem

₹ Lost Every Day Without Knowing It

Indian dairy farmers operate on instinct, not data. Every missed heat cycle, untracked milk drop, or poor selling decision silently erodes income.

01

Missed Breeding Cycles

A missed heat cycle = 21 days lost + lost calf + lost future milk. Most farmers have no system to track or predict the next cycle.

₹8,000+
02

No Milk Production Tracking

Farmers guess their weekly output. Without daily records, they can't detect gradual yield decline from health issues or poor feed until it's too late.

20% avg loss
03

Poor Cattle Price Decisions

When to buy, when to sell, and at what price? Without market data or cattle history, farmers consistently leave money on the table.

No benchmark
04

Zero Digital Tools

Existing farm apps are designed for desktop-first, English-speaking, tech-literate users. They fail rural India completely.

0 usable apps
05

Delayed Health Alerts

By the time a farmer notices a sick cow, 3–5 days of milk yield are already lost. Early signals go undetected without a tracking system.

3–5 days lost
06

No Financial Visibility

Farmers have no clear picture of monthly income, cost of cattle maintenance, or return on feed investment. Decision-making is entirely intuitive.

UX Philosophy

Built for Low Literacy. Loved by All.

Rural India's farmers are intelligent, resourceful people — but many have limited formal literacy and low digital exposure. Every UX decision is made with this reality at the center.

No long forms. No English labels. No multi-step flows. Every feature must pass the test: can a farmer with a 4th grade education use this in 30 seconds, in sunlight, while holding a bucket?

🈺

Hindi & Regional First

UI copy in Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi. No English unless absolutely unavoidable.

🖼️

Icon-Based UI

Every action communicates via icon + label. Text is supplementary, not primary.

⌨️

Minimal Typing

Replace text input with selection, voice, or WhatsApp. Tapping beats typing.

🔲

Large Tap Targets

Buttons and inputs sized for hands that work outdoors. No tiny checkboxes.

R1

Max 2–3 Steps Per Action

If a farmer needs more than 3 taps to log milk or add a breeding record, we redesign until it's under 3.

R2

Replace Typing with Selection

Dropdowns, toggles, numeric steppers, quick-select chips. Forms are a last resort, not a default.

R3

Show Immediate Value After Input

After logging milk, show today's total and earnings estimate immediately. Instant reward loop drives retention.

R4

Offline First, Always

Every action queues locally and syncs silently. Farmers in 2G/no-signal areas can still log all data without friction.

R5

Actions Over Data

Every screen ends with a clear next step. No data graveyard screens — every view tells the farmer what to do next.

R6

Sub-60 Second Onboarding

First cow added and first milk logged in under 60 seconds from app open. First value delivered before the second minute.

Product Differentiators

A Decision Engine, Not a Data App.

Every feature exists to prompt action, not display numbers. The system watches data — the farmer acts on insights.

🎯

Smart Milk Drop Detection

Tracks per-cattle baseline and fires an alert when yield drops more than 15%. Not a chart — a specific recommendation: "Check Lakshmi's feed or schedule vet."

🌡️

Heat Cycle Prediction Engine

Tracks 21-day cycle history per cow and sends a 24-48hr pre-warning. The most underserved and highest-value alert in Indian dairy farming. One miss = ₹8,000+ loss.

💬

WhatsApp Native Entry

Farmer types "Ganga ne 8 litre diya" in WhatsApp. System parses, stores the milk record, and replies with today's total. Zero app opens required for daily logging.

🤰

Calving Preparation Alerts

"Ganga 3 din mein baccha degi — dawai, saaman aur jagah tayaar rakhein." Specific, actionable, timed. Not a due date notification — a preparation checklist.

💰

Real Income Tracking

Weekly and monthly earning summaries tied directly to the data the farmer logged. Makes the value of using the app visible and tangible, driving retention.

🗂️

Breeding Data Goldmine

Every heat cycle, pregnancy, calving recorded becomes a scientific dataset. Long-term: breed performance benchmarks, regional heat prediction models, veterinary research data.

Offline Sync Architecture

  • All writes queue locally first (SQLite)
  • Background sync on connectivity restore
  • Conflict resolution via timestamp + server-wins
  • Optimistic UI — no waiting for network
  • Sync status indicator in app header

WhatsApp NLP Pipeline

  • Incoming message hits webhook endpoint
  • Intent parser identifies action (log, query, alert)
  • Entity extractor finds cow name + quantity
  • Creates DB record, returns Hindi confirmation
  • Supports Hindi, Gujarati, mixed language

Smart Alert Engine

  • Bull queue cron evaluates cattle data nightly
  • Heat cycle model fires 24–48hr pre-warning
  • Milk drop detector checks 3-day rolling average
  • Calving ETA = pregnancy record + 280-day calc
  • Delivers via FCM push + WhatsApp message
